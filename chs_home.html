Important Notice Regarding Upcoming Appointments and Covid

In order to better protect the safety of it's patients and employees, Carolina Health Specialists are currently requiring all patients to wear some form of face covering before entering any Carolina Health Specialists facility. Medical masks are preferred. Any patient who arrives without a form of face covering will be turned away at the door. Additionally, in an attempt to limit the number of people in our waiting rooms in order to better adhere to social distancing guidelines, Carolina Health Specialists will not allow visitors to accompany patients inside the building for their appointments. Therefore, we recommend that all patients attend their appointments by themselves. However, if that is not possible, we will require all visitors who accompany patients to their appointment to remain outside of the building and wait in the parking lot. Exceptions will be made for visitors of patients who require assistance due to medical reasons. If a visitor is allowed into the building, they must also wear some form of face covering as described above. Thank you in advance for your understanding and for working with us to ensure the safety of our patients and staff. We do not currently have the COVID vaccine. The government is in charge of distributing the vaccine and setting the order of who receives the vaccine and when. Please see the SC DHEC website for more information on the distribution and the timetable for different groups to receive the vaccine. We have applied to be a vaccine administration site. We will send out updates once they become available.
